# Trading Playbook (Starter, Non-Financial-Advice)

## Core principles
- Risk first, returns second.
- Position sizing before entry logic.
- Journal every trade with hypothesis + postmortem.

## Daily routine
- Pre-market: scenarios + invalidation levels.
- During market: execute plan, avoid impulse trades.
- Post-market: review stats, mistakes, and rule adherence.

## Risk guardrails (template)
- Max risk per trade: ___%
- Max daily loss: ___R
- Max concurrent correlated exposure: ___%
